## Project Title: Captain: 7 Seas
## Project Members: Noah Gibb, Kevin Fudge, Ruben Gonzalez
## Platform: VR
## Hardware Requirements: Oculus Quest 2
## Target Audience: Anyone who likes pirates and combat
## Game Synopsis: You're the captain of a pirate style ship, you're tasked with steering your ship to avoid obstacles and defending it against enemies trying to board and steal your cargo.
