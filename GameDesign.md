# Game Design Document
## 1. Premise: Balance steering your ship to avoid obstacles and defending your ship against whoever is boarding.

## 2. Story/Narrative: You're the captain of a ship cominf back from retreiving cargo when you're attacked by another crew that wants to steal your cargo, you must defend your cargo while also steering your ship to avoid obstacles.

## 3. Timeline: Our plan for development will be working on multiple parts and features of this game simultaneously, 

## 4. Main Goal: Bring your ship back to port while protecting your cargo and without crashing.

## 5. Main Character: You play as a generic grizzled sea captain. The only one able to defend the ship against the boarding parties of your enemies.

## 6. World/Environments: The top deck of a classic merchant ship, mix between realistic and cartoon styles
